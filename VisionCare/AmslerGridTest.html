Amsler Grid Test

Cover one eye, focus on the central dot, and use your mouse to draw any wavy, blurry, or missing lines you see.

Result Legend

  • RED = Worsening

    Highlights new distortions you did not have on your last test.

  • GREEN = Improving

    Highlights areas that were distorted, but now appear clear.

  • BLACK = Stable

    This is your drawing of a distortion that has not changed.

If you see new RED areas, please consult your eye care professional.
